About Me

// BIO

An architect driven by the principles of brutalism, I create spaces that are honest, functional, and monumental. My work strips away the superfluous to reveal the raw beauty of structure and material.

// PHILOSOPHY

Architecture should be a testament to its time and place, unapologetic in its form. I focus on exposed concrete, steel, and glass to craft environments that are both imposing and deeply human.
